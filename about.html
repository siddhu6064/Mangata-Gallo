Our Story

A legacy of craftsmanship, passion, and timeless elegance

The Beginning of Mangata & Gallo

2018

The Dream Takes Shape

Master jeweler Mariana graduates from the prestigious Gemological Institute with specialized training in diamond cutting and metal smithing. Her vision: to create a jewelry house that celebrates love's most precious moments with uncompromising craftsmanship.

2019

Austin Welcomes Mangata & Gallo

Mariana opens her first boutique atelier in Austin's historic arts district. The intimate space combines a showroom with a working atelier, allowing clients to witness the magic of jewelry creation firsthand. The name "Mangata" - Swedish for the road-like reflection of the moon on water - perfectly captures the ethereal beauty of the pieces created here.

2020

Growing Recognition

Despite global challenges, Mangata & Gallo gains recognition for exceptional custom engagement rings. Word spreads through Austin and beyond about the personalized service and exquisite craftsmanship. Mariana begins collaborating with select local artisans, expanding the atelier's capabilities while maintaining its intimate, personal touch.

2021

Expanding Horizons

The launch of our online presence brings Mangata & Gallo to clients across Texas and beyond. Our commitment to sustainable and ethical practices earns certification from leading responsible jewelry organizations. The team grows to include master goldsmiths and gemologists, each sharing Mariana's passion for excellence.

2022

Heritage Collection Launch

Inspired by vintage European techniques, Mariana introduces the Heritage Collection - pieces that honor traditional craftsmanship while embracing contemporary aesthetics. The collection receives acclaim from jewelry enthusiasts and establishes Mangata & Gallo as a destination for collectors seeking unique, investment-quality pieces.

2023-2025

A Legacy Continues

Today, Mangata & Gallo stands as Austin's premier destination for luxury jewelry and custom design. With over 500 custom pieces created and countless love stories celebrated, we continue to grow while staying true to our founding principles: exceptional craftsmanship, personalized service, and ethical practices. Our atelier has become a pilgrimage site for couples seeking rings as unique as their love stories.

Meet Master Jeweler Mariana

Mariana's journey into jewelry began in childhood, watching her grandmother's delicate hands restore antique pieces in a small European workshop. This early exposure to the intersection of artistry and emotion would shape her entire career.

After formal training at world-renowned institutions and apprenticeships with master craftspeople across Europe, Mariana brought her expertise to Austin with a mission: to create jewelry that captures not just beauty, but the essence of human connection.

"Every piece tells a story," Mariana explains. "My role is to listen to that story and translate it into something tangible, something that will carry that emotion forward through generations."

Her philosophy combines traditional European techniques with innovative modern approaches, ensuring each piece meets the highest standards of both artistry and durability. Under her guidance, Mangata & Gallo has become synonymous with excellence in custom jewelry design.

"Jewelry is not just adornment - it's a vessel for memories, a symbol of promises, and a celebration of life's most precious moments." - Mariana
